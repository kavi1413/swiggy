-INDUSTRY PIONEER-




Being among the first few entrants, Swiggy has successfully pioneered the hyperlocal
commerce industry in India, launching Food Delivery in 2014 and Quick Commerce
in 2020. Due to the pioneering status of Swiggy, it is well-recognised as
a leader in innovation in hyperlocal commerce and a category-defining brand; trusted,
intuitive and deeply embedded in the daily routines of millions of Indians.
